Motivate yourself.
Literally.

Hana helps motivate you by utilizing the one thing standing in your way. Yourself.

Fill out the Questionnaire now.

The 6-step process.

  1. Fill out the Questionnaire exploring exactly why you want to achieve your goal.
  2. Receive one of your answers in an email the next day.
  3. Get motivated and get to work!
  4. Reply to the email with a short daily log.
  5. Receive another one of your answers—along with your log from the day before—the following day.
  6. Wash, rinse, and repeat.

Who made this?

Hi. My name's Min Jun, but call me Eric. I'm probly just like you, with many dreams, goals, and aspirations.

I have a problem though, and that's motivation. Quotes by famous people have never really done it for me. Neither has reading thousands of blog posts on the subject. Nor fancy to-do lists or apps.

Hana is my attempt at solving, or at least alleviating this problem just a little bit.

It is my belief that nothing motivates better than reading your own heartfelt answers to key questions regarding your goal, along with your own words about the progress you've made thus far.

Give it a shot. Right now, in its bare form, Hana is completely free of charge. But stay tuned. I'm working on web and mobile apps to make things a lot more convenient and simple to use.
