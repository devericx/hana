The 6-step process.

  1. Fill out the Questionnaire exploring exactly why you want to achieve your goal.
  2. Receive one of your answers in an email the next day.
  3. Get motivated and get to work!
  4. Reply to the email with a short daily log.
  5. Receive another one of your answers—along with your log from the day before—the following day.
  6. Wash, rinse, and repeat.

Why?

Motivational quotes help very little. To-do lists and calendars just add more things to do and not enough reasons to do them.

So what works? Accountability. Not just to others, but to yourself, your past self.

Hana holds you accountable by reminding you every day the specific reasons why you want to achieve your goal.
